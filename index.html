Age-normalized: cit ÷ years — ranked by percentile within the batch. ⓘ see example
Why age-normalize?
A 2010 paper has had more years to accumulate citations — raw counts always favor older papers.
2010 paper
500 cit ÷ 15 yrs = 33/yrscore 15
2023 paper
80 cit ÷ 2 yrs = 40/yr ↑score 20
Fewer raw citations — but faster accumulation. Without normalization the 2010 paper would always win.
